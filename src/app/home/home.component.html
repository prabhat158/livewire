About Livewire

Welcome to livewire

LiveWire is the celebration of independent music in India and aims to bring out the most unique music from various parts of our nation.
Livewire - the oldest and biggest semi-professional band launchpad has been instrumental in promoting independent music scene in India for the past 39 years.
Over the years, Livewire has seen the birth of some of the country's greatest bands like Parikrama, The F16s, Pentagram, Motherjane, Zero and Goddess Gagged. The revamped model of LiveWire is open not just rock and metal but also the most genre-bending sounds. The language of performance is not a barrier till the band is performing original compositions.
